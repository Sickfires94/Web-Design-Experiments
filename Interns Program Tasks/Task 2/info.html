COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.

The disease causes respitory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases, difficulty breating. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face, adn avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.

How it spreads

Coronavirus deisease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cought or sneeze. It aslso spreads whean a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes, nose, or mouth.
